# Shipping Information

## Standard Shipping
- **Estimated delivery:** 5–7 business days
- **Cost:** Free on orders over $50; $4.99 for orders under $50
- **Carrier:** USPS, FedEx, or UPS (assigned automatically)

## Express Shipping
- **Estimated delivery:** 2–3 business days
- **Cost:** $12.99 flat rate
- **Carrier:** FedEx or UPS

## Overnight Shipping
- **Estimated delivery:** Next business day (if ordered before 2 PM EST)
- **Cost:** $24.99 flat rate
- **Carrier:** FedEx Priority Overnight

## International Shipping
- **Estimated delivery:** 10–21 business days
- **Cost:** Calculated at checkout based on destination and weight
- **Note:** Customers are responsible for any customs fees or import duties.

## Order Processing Time
All orders are processed within **1–2 business days** of purchase. Orders placed on weekends or holidays are processed the next business day.

## Tracking Your Order
Once shipped, you'll receive a tracking number via email. Track your package at the carrier's website or in your account dashboard.

## Late Deliveries
If your order hasn't arrived within the estimated window:
1. Check the tracking number for the latest status.
2. Allow 1–2 extra business days for carrier delays.
3. If still not delivered, contact support with your order number.

## Lost Packages
If the tracking shows "Delivered" but you haven't received your package:
1. Check with neighbors and around your property.
2. Wait 24 hours — packages are sometimes marked delivered early.
3. Contact support — we'll file a carrier claim and send a replacement or refund.
