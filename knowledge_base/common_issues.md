# Common Issues & Resolutions

## Damaged Item
**What to do:** Take photos of the damaged item and packaging. Contact support with your order number and photos. We'll arrange a replacement or full refund without requiring you to return the item.

**Typical resolution time:** 1–2 business days after photos are received.

## Wrong Item Received
**What to do:** Contact support immediately with your order number and a description/photo of the wrong item. We'll ship the correct item with express shipping at no charge and provide a prepaid return label for the wrong item.

**Typical resolution time:** Correct item ships within 1 business day.

## Late Delivery
**What to do:** First, check your tracking number. If your package is still in transit, wait the full estimated window plus 2 business days. If still not arrived, contact support.

**When we can help:** We can file a carrier claim after 7 business days past the estimated delivery date for domestic orders.

## Missing Item in Order
**What to do:** If part of your order is missing, check if it was shipped in a separate package (you'd have multiple tracking numbers). If only one package was expected, contact support with photos of what you received.

**Typical resolution time:** Missing item ships within 1–2 business days.

## Billing Issues
**Duplicate Charges:** If you were charged twice, we'll verify and issue a refund for the duplicate charge within 3–5 business days.

**Unauthorized Charge:** Contact your bank to dispute and contact us simultaneously. We'll investigate from our side.

**Coupon Not Applied:** If a discount code didn't apply at checkout, contact support within 48 hours of ordering with the code. We'll issue the discount as store credit.

## Account & Login Issues
**Forgot Password:** Use the "Forgot Password" link on the login page to reset via email.

**Order Not Showing:** Log out and back in. If the order doesn't appear, contact support with your confirmation email.

## Item No Longer Available
If your ordered item becomes out of stock after your purchase, you'll receive an email with options: wait for restock, substitute with a similar item, or receive a full refund.
