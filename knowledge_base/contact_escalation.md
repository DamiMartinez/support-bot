# Contact & Escalation Procedures

## Standard Support Channels

### Live Chat
- **Hours:** Monday–Friday 9 AM–6 PM EST, Saturday 10 AM–4 PM EST
- **Access:** Click "Chat Now" on the website or in the app
- **Typical wait time:** Under 2 minutes during business hours

### Email Support
- **Address:** support@orbio-store.example.com
- **Response time:** Within 24 hours on business days
- **Best for:** Complex issues requiring documentation, photos, or order history

### Phone Support
- **Number:** 1-800-ORBIO-01 (1-800-672-4601)
- **Hours:** Monday–Friday 8 AM–8 PM EST
- **Best for:** Urgent billing issues or account security concerns

## Escalation Path

### Tier 1 — Standard Support (first contact)
Handles: order status, returns, shipping questions, billing inquiries, common issues

### Tier 2 — Senior Support (escalated by Tier 1)
Handles: High-value order disputes ($200+), repeated failed resolutions, legal or fraud concerns
**Typical escalation trigger:** Issue unresolved after 2 contacts, or customer requests escalation

### Tier 3 — Management (extreme cases)
Handles: Formal complaints, media inquiries, regulatory/legal matters
**Access:** Via written request to legal@orbio-store.example.com

## When to Escalate
An issue should be escalated to Tier 2 when:
- The customer has contacted support 2+ times for the same issue
- The order value exceeds $200
- A carrier claim has been denied
- The customer reports safety concerns about the product
- The customer mentions legal action or regulatory complaints

## Urgent / High Priority Issues
Issues marked **high urgency** are routed to the next available senior agent and must receive a first response within 2 hours during business hours.
