# Returns & Refund Policy

## Return Window
Customers have **30 days** from the date of delivery to initiate a return. Items must be unused, in their original packaging, and accompanied by a receipt or order confirmation.

## Eligibility
Eligible for return:
- Unopened or unused items in original condition
- Damaged or defective items (any timeframe)
- Wrong items received

Not eligible for return:
- Digital products and software licenses
- Perishable goods
- Items marked "Final Sale"
- Personalized or custom-made items

## Refund Processing
Once we receive and inspect the returned item, refunds are processed within **5–7 business days** to the original payment method. Customers will receive an email confirmation when the refund is issued.

## How to Initiate a Return
1. Contact support with your order number and reason for return.
2. Our team will send a prepaid return shipping label within 24 hours.
3. Pack the item securely and drop it off at any authorized carrier location.
4. Track your return using the label's tracking number.

## Exchanges
We offer direct exchanges for wrong-size or wrong-color issues. Processing time is the same as returns (5–7 business days after receipt).

## Damaged Items
If your item arrived damaged, please take photos immediately and contact support. We will either send a replacement or issue a full refund — no need to return the damaged item in most cases.
